Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is a beloved Filipino dish known for its rich, savory, and slightly tangy flavor. This recipe features tender pork simmered in a fragrant blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper, creating a deep umami taste with a hint of zest. As it slowly cooks, the meat absorbs the bold flavors, resulting in a dish that is both hearty and comforting.
